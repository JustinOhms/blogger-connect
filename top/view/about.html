Origin

I am using google blogger for quite long time [ almost two and half years ]. It's awesome service that we are getting for free. But it's editor is quite problmatic, it adds <div> and <br> unexpectldy. So its hard to manage view of post.

I tried to find online service or products which are giving easy editor like write.as. But don't find any. So, I decided to build one for me.

Ingredients

Blogger editor is built on top of Blogger API v3, Golang And VueJS. VueJS-wyswyg editor and VueBlock used for UI and editor.
